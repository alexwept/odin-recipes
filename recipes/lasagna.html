Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is an Italian dish and Garfield's favourite food. It was first shown on the July 15th of 1978 comic strip, where Garfield desribed lasagna as "nature's most perfect food".

Historically, especially in the early days of the Garfield strip, lasagna has been Garfield's food of choice. This holds true in his TV shows, Garfield and Friends, and The Garfield Show, where the food has numerous references and appearances as Garfield's favourite food.

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90 g prosciutto
  • 200 ml hot beef stock
  • 1 whole nutmeg
  • 300 g fresh lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125 g ball mozzarella
